Our Mission

We as a community will,
  • Be a centre for sharing stories about impactful activities
    , best practices, and ways tosupport the Sustainable Development Goals
  • Serve as a platform for the UN to engage the MUN community,
    share information, and serve as a source of inspiration.
  • Provide learning opportunities for students that are centred on
    the SDGs and bring together people from all over the world.
  • Organize and host student-planned and executed events that
    promote further sharing and collaboration on the SDGs.
